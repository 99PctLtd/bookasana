faq

You got questions? we got answers.

Q1. Who are we?

We are an independent agency that recognize the increasing frustration you experience while trying to reserve a mat in a yoga class with your favorite teacher. We are here to offer an alternative, attempting to reduce that level of stress by providing a booking service to reserve classes on your behave.

Q2. What kind of service do you provide?

Upon successful registration, which require valid login with Pure, you are no longer bounded by that scroll and click competition at 9:00 every morning. You will be able to list any class* as soon as the schedule becomes available**.

Here are a few ideas of what the above can possibly mean:

  • No more pressure to be around your phone or computer at 9:00 to get ready to refresh the app/page.
  • No more forgetting to reserve a mat at 9:00 while you are stuck in early morning meeting, or occupied by all the necessary house work.
  • No more worrying class fill up while you are waiting for slow internet connection during your morning commute.
  • No more checking your wait list position, praying to move up hours before class start.

While we won't be able to free up mat in classes within two days, we will be able to help you get into any class with advance booking.

YES! We are confident that we can always reserve the class you desire, and we will NOT charge you unless you get a mat. We won't even charge for getting you on the wait list, we consider that a failure on our part. Only when the booking is successful, we will deduct from your balance the amount of token you spend on the class.

All in all, you will be able to list your favorite class with us, days before reservation opens, expecting you will always be able to get a mat in the most popular class. Why stress over it everyday while you can be over with it once a week stress free?


* You can only reserve classes in the specific centers per contract signed with Pure.

** Classes and staffs do change occasionally after schedule is released. While we make our best effort to keep up with the changes, you hold the responsibility to ensure the latest class schedule remain align with your needs.

Q3. Why do I need to provide my Pure login?

As we are an independent agency, we do not have direct access to the scheduling app/page. Like you, we also need your valid login in order to access the schedule and reserve a class. By using our service, you are giving us permission to reserve class on your behalf.

Q4. What would you do with my personal information?

Your privacy is very import to us, we take the security of your personal information very seriously, and strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect it.

The data we collect may be used, but is not limited to:

  • To provide and maintain the Service
  • To notify you about changes to our Service
  • To allow you to participate in interactive features of our Service when you choose to do so
  • To provide customer care and support
  • To provide analysis or valuable information so that we can improve the Service
  • To monitor the usage of the Service
  • To detect, prevent and address technical issues

For more details you may refer to our privacy policy.

Q5. How does bookasana work?

You would receive 5 free tokens upon successful registration.

Each advance reservation will require a minimum of 1 token to add to reservation list. Once a class is added to your reservation list, we will proceed to reserve that class for you when the appropriate time comes (9:00 two days before class).

In the case of extremely popular classes, increasing the amount of token you spend in a class will increase your priority and chance of getting a mat.

Indeed, you can use our system to reserve for classes within two days, which does not require any token.
